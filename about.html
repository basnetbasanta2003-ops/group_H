How It Started

Our Story

Basanta Basnet Company was founded with one simple goal — to give businesses access to the same quality of technology and support that larger companies enjoy. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted team of professionals serving clients all over the UK.

We have always believed that good work speaks for itself. We do not chase quick wins. We build long-term relationships with our clients based on trust, honesty and consistent results.
